How it works

The calculator estimates cylinder performance using bore, rod, stroke, pressure, and flow. Extend force is pressure multiplied by piston area. Retract force uses the annulus area (piston minus rod area). Volume and speed are derived from the same geometry and the available flow rate.

Notes for offline use

  • All calculations are performed locally in the browser.
  • Use the unit selector to switch between metric and imperial values.
  • The results update instantly as you modify the inputs.
